Noveen Morris

Noveen Morris is a Singer, Songwriter and Music Producer associated with Punjabi music. He was born in Lahore, Pakistan and raised in Washinton D.C U.S.a. Noveen Started his career with the songs ‘Zindagi Ka Safar’ in 2015. Later he came out with ‘Preetaan’,’Tere Piche’, ‘Nai Dil Lagda’, ’66 East Vibes’, ‘Sohnea’ and recently collaborated with Bohemia the Punjabi Rapper on a song Kaisa Nasha with 2 million views on Youtube.
